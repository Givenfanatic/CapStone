Welcome to a tribute page for the most historic English football team.

Testimonies by former players and managers.

Cristiano Ronaldo

"At United, there are great traditions, which you can't buy in one or two years. They are created by victories. You need to prove again and again that you are better than the others. Manchester United have always done this, and are still doing it, so they are the best."

Patrice Evra

“It's a special club. It's got history. When I slip on the Manchester United shirt, it's like I'm wearing its past. So you have to sacrifice yourself for this club.”

Jose Mourinho

“If you want me to rule out ever being Manchester United manager, I can't. Special clubs need special managers, so in theory it could work.”

Matt Busby

“I never wanted Manchester United to be second to anybody. Only the best would be good enough.”

Robin Van Persie

“Manchester United breathe football. When I have to make hard decisions, I always listen to the little boy inside me and what he wants. That little boy was screaming for United.”

Dimitar Berbatov

“My mind was always set on joining Manchester United. How can you not go to United? I don't play for money. I play for glory and winning championships. I'm happy here. I hear people complain about the weather, there's nothing to do and the food. But, for me, it's not like that. I play for a club I love, the biggest club, and everything else doesn't matter.”

Sir Alex Ferguson

“Manchester United might not win Premier League every year, but we'd always be up there competing for it every year.”

Romelu Lukaku

“At Manchester United, we play to win. That's my first mentality when I get onto the pitch. Then everything else comes with it.”

Eric Cantona

“When I think football, I think Manchester United. I still support United and always will. I will die with them in my heart.”

Sir Matt Busby

“At United we strive for perfection and if we fail we might just have to settle for excellence.”
